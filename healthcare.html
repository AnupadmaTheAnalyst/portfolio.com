This Power BI dashboard analyzes healthcare billing, patient demographics, treatment data, and insurance coverage. The goal: empower administrators to make informed decisions that reduce costs, improve service quality, and optimize revenue.

üìä Case Study: Improving Financial & Operational Performance

Business Challenge

Healthcare leaders lacked unified visibility into billing trends, departmental performance, insurance impact, and weekday vs weekend service usage. Fragmented spreadsheets made analysis slow and inaccurate.

Key Questions

  • Which departments generate the most revenue?
  • How much do insurance providers cover?
  • Why does billing fluctuate across months, years, and days?
  • Where are opportunities for cost optimization?

Analytical Approach

  • Built a star-schema model (FactVisits + Dimensions)
  • Transformed data in Power Query (cleaning, merging, shaping)
  • Developed DAX measures for KPI calculations
  • Designed Light & Dark dashboards for better usability
  • Created separate Trend and Detail dashboards

Key Insights

  • 66% of billing is covered by insurance.
  • Weekday revenue is 60% higher than weekends.
  • Cardiology + Orthopedics contribute 70% of total billing.
  • YoY billing dropped 29.6% due to seasonal decline.
  • Neurology shows steady positive growth (+13.6%).

Business Impact

The insights help administrators allocate resources effectively, strengthen high-revenue departments, and prepare targeted operational improvements. Weekend service expansion and focused patient engagement can improve Q3‚ÄìQ4 performance.
